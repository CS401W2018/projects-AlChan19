About Paramore

Paramore is an American rock band formed in 2004 in Franklin, Tennessee. The band is known for its energetic performances, emotional lyrics, and genre-blending sound that evolved from pop punk to alternative rock influences.

Over the years, Paramore has undergone lineup changes but remains one of the most influential alternative rock bands of the 21st century.
