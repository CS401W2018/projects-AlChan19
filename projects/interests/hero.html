My Hero

Interest | Hero

I love Hayley Williams from Paramore so much. Her voice is so strong and full of emotion, and every time I listen to her sing, I feel alive and completely in the moment. Whether I am happy, sad, or just lost in thought, her voice always brings me comfort. I have also listened to her solo songs, and they are just as amazing. When Paramore went on a break, I felt really sentimental because their music had been a big part of my life. It made me so happy when they finally released a new single and album last year. One of my biggest dreams is to watch them perform live someday.
