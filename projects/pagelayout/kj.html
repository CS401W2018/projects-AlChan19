My Favorite Valorant Agents

Killjoy Gekko Sage Clove

An agent in Valorant named Klara Böhringer who originated in Germany with the codename 'Killjoy'. She is a young tech user and a genius, who uses various gadgets and bots of her own invention in combat.

Killjoy is a sentinel agent who uses her inventions to control enemies and areas. Her abilities include Nanoswarm and Alarmbot which are her basic abilities, Turret which is her signature ability, and Lockdown which is her ultimate ability.

She is my main agent to use because she is easy to play, her abilities are excellent for defensive plays, and her skills are fun to use.

  • Nanoswarm – A grenade-like device that releases nanobots that damage enemies over time.
  • Alarmbot – A bot that hunts down enemies within a radius, makes enemies vulnerable to more damage, and can be recalled.
  • Turret – A mini-turret that fires at enemies in its line of sight.
  • Lockdown – A device that traps enemies in a large area for eight seconds making them detained and unable to move.
