What are special entity characters?

Special entity characters are used to display reserved characters in HTML. They cannot be easily typed in a standard keyboard. It allows users to add icons, geometric shapes, mathematical operators, and so on.

What are phrase tags?

Phrase tags are also known as semantic tags or inline elements that is used to indicate the meaning or purpose of a block of text.
