Engagement - Form

  1. The "action" attribute in the form tag specifies the URL where the form data will be sent when the form is submitted.
  2. The "method" attribute in the form tag specifies how the form data will be sent to the server (either using "GET" or "POST").
  3. The difference between "POST" and "GET":
    • "GET" sends data as part of the URL and is mainly used for retrieving data, making it visible in the URL.
    • "POST" sends data in the body of the request, which is hidden and more secure for sensitive or larger data submissions.
