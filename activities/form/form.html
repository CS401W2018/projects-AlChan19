Engagement - Form Styling

  1. What are different ways to style your form?
    • You can use CSS to change colors, fonts, and spacing.
    • Use padding and margins to make the form look neat.
    • Change input box sizes to match the design.
    • Use borders and background colors to highlight sections.
  2. Why does the professor like to use flex?
    • Flex makes it easy to align form elements.
    • It helps position inputs, labels, and buttons neatly.
    • It works well on different screen sizes.
    • It makes forms look more organized with less code.
