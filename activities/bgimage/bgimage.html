Background Images

When should you use a background image, and when should you use the IMG tag?

– You should use a background image when the picture is only for decoration, like patterns, textures or designs that are not important for the main content. You should use the IMG tag when the image is important for the content, like photos, logos or pictures that help explain something visually.

  • Repeating – A background image that repeats over and over to fill the space.
  • Non-repeating – A background image that appears only once and does not repeat.
  • UL images – Images used inside a list (like icons or bullet points) to style list items.
